Frequently Asked Questions

PersianParcel is an innovative platform designed to facilitate international package delivery by connecting senders with trusted travelers. Our service allows you to send or receive packages across borders by leveraging a network of travelers who are heading to or from your desired destinations.

We aim to make international shipping more efficient, cost-effective, and secure. Whether you need to send a gift, important documents, or personal belongings, PersianParcel provides a reliable solution by connecting you with vetted travelers who can transport your package safely.

Signing up is easy! Visit our Sign Up page and fill out the registration form with your basic information, including your name, email address, phone number, and a secure password. You'll also need to verify your email address to complete the registration process.

Once registered, you can set up your profile, specify whether you are a sender or traveler, and start using our platform to create listings and connect with other users.

Our platform uses a sophisticated matching algorithm to connect senders and travelers. When you create a listing, our system searches for travelers who are heading to or from your specified destination. We consider factors such as travel dates, routes, and user ratings to ensure the best match.

You will receive notifications about potential matches, and you can review traveler profiles or sender requests to choose the most suitable option for your needs. Once a match is confirmed, you can coordinate the details through our platform.

Yes, security is a top priority for us. All transactions are protected by our escrow payment system. When you make a payment, the funds are held securely until the package is delivered and confirmed. This ensures that both parties are protected and that the transaction is fair.

Additionally, we implement advanced encryption and security protocols to safeguard your personal and financial information throughout the entire process.

If your package is delayed, please take the following steps:

  • Check the tracking status on our platform to see if there are any updates or notifications.
  • Contact the traveler or sender through our messaging system to inquire about the status of the package.
  • If you cannot resolve the issue with the traveler or sender, reach out to our support team with details of the delay. We will assist you in tracking the package and addressing any issues that may have occurred.

We strive to ensure timely deliveries and will work with you to resolve any problems as quickly as possible.
